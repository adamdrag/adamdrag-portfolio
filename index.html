Since a young age I’ve had a passion for problem-solving, technology and creative design and continuous learning. Over the last few years I’ve developed applications as a hobby but a recent project of creating our company’s Intranet site and supporting databases has ignited my desire to pursue a career in Full Stack Web Development.


I completed the Full Stack Developer, Coding Boot Camp at the University of Arizona in January 2019 and am now attending IntraEdge's entry level programmer training course.


My skills are broad, from UI and UX design to back-end development and I love building websites and mobile apps. My goal on any project is to create a balance between technical functionality and visual elements for all the usable, adaptable and understandable needs of the user. With a refined sense of aesthetics, I’m passionate about developing engaging, highly functional web applications that have a simple, clean and distinct design.


As a naturally inquisitive, life-long learner with a passion for problem-solving, I won’t put a problem, project or challenge down until I’ve resolved it. I’m exceptionally skilled at making things work with an obsessive attention to detail. My application build process involves the use of tools such as Sass, Grunt, Gulp, Bower, and NPM to speed up development.


When I’m not coding or designing, you’ll find me hiking, swimming or reading books. I also have a bachelor’s degree in Behavioral Psychology, a diploma in Marketing and certification in Information Technology.
